Site Plan

Site Name

Rescue the Turtles CR The name reflects our mission to protect sea turtles. It is easy to remember and clearly indicate that the project is based in Costa Rica.

Site Purpose

This website provides educational information about sea turtles to help visitors connect emotionally with their cause. Its main goal is to attract people who want to volunteer or donate to support turtle conservation. We offer different volunteer plans that allow participants to contribute directly to saving turtles while enjoying a meaningful experience.

Scenarios

  • How can I participate in saving the turtles?
  • If I cannot volunteer in person, how else can I help?
  • What is the best way to enjoy a trip and support a good cause at the same time?
